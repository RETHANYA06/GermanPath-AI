# Goethe A1 Exam Format

## Goal

The Goethe-Zertifikat A1 certifies that a learner can communicate in simple everyday situations using basic German.

---

## Total Sections

1. Reading (Lesen)
2. Listening (Hören)
3. Writing (Schreiben)
4. Speaking (Sprechen)

---

## Reading (Lesen)

Duration: Approximately 25 minutes

Skills Tested:
- Understanding short texts
- Notices and advertisements
- Emails and messages
- Everyday information

Question Types:
- Multiple choice
- True/False
- Matching

---

## Listening (Hören)

Duration: Approximately 20 minutes

Skills Tested:
- Understanding simple conversations
- Public announcements
- Phone messages
- Daily communication

Question Types:
- Multiple choice
- True/False
- Matching

---

## Writing (Schreiben)

Duration: Approximately 20 minutes

Skills Tested:
- Filling forms
- Writing short emails
- Writing simple messages

Typical Length:
20–40 words

---

## Speaking (Sprechen)

Duration: Approximately 15 minutes

Parts:

1. Introduce Yourself
2. Ask Questions
3. Respond to Questions

Topics:
- Name
- Age
- Country
- Occupation
- Hobbies
- Family

---

## Passing Criteria

Minimum score required:
60%

---

## Target Learner

Absolute beginner to elementary German learner.
